Learn More
Features

Identify and target your specific stuttering symptoms through our individual and community modes.

Diagnosis

Our novel deep and supervised learning AI models can pinpoint the severity of various stuttering symptoms in a matter of seconds from a short recording of your speech.

Training

Receive personalized training exercise suggestions catered to your unique disfluency pattern that actively respond to your changing improvement rate.

Community

Become a part of the worldwide stuttering community through an inclusive forum, a motivational storyboard, and team-centered games and exercises.
